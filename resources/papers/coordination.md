# Coordination-Focused Resources

- Why Do Multi-Agent LLM Systems Fail?: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.13657 — failure taxonomy for coordination breakdowns, role mismatch, and termination problems.
- M-MAD: https://aclanthology.org/2025.acl-long.351/ — debate-style coordination turned into a concrete evaluation system with code/data.
- Multiple LLM Agents Debate for Equitable Cultural Alignment: https://aclanthology.org/2025.acl-long.1210/ — coordination plus fairness/alignment case for culturally grounded tasks.
- DEBATE: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25110 — opinion-dynamics benchmark for role-playing MAS, useful when social interaction realism matters.
- Coordinated LLM multi-agent systems for collaborative question-answer generation: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.knosys.2025.114627 — application-level collaborative QA rather than pure infrastructure or coding.
